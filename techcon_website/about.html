History

Since its inception in 2010, TechCon has grown from a modest regional meetup into one of the most influential global technology conferences. What began as a gathering of passionate developers in a small co-working space has transformed into a multi-day international summit attracting thousands of participants— from engineers and entrepreneurs to researchers and policymakers. Over the years, TechCon has been a launchpad for breakthrough ideas, emerging startups, and industry-defining collaborations across AI, cloud computing, cybersecurity, IoT, and more.

Our Mission

TechCon’s mission is to foster a dynamic environment for technology professionals to exchange knowledge, ignite innovation, and inspire the next generation of tech leaders. We aim to create inclusive spaces for meaningful dialogue, practical learning, and long-term impact in the digital age.
